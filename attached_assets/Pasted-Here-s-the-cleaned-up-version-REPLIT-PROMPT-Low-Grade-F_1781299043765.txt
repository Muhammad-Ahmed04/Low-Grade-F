**REPLIT PROMPT: Low Grade Films (LGF) Website**

---

Build a single-page, fully responsive website for **Low Grade Films (LGF)** — a premium videography and photography company specializing in automobile, tactical/guns, and commercial brand content. The site should feel like a high-end automotive brand meets a cinematic production house.

---

### TECH STACK
- HTML, CSS, Vanilla JS (no frameworks needed)
- GSAP for scroll animations (load from CDN)
- Google Fonts

---

### BRAND & VISUAL IDENTITY

**Colors:**
- Background: `#000000` (pure black)
- Primary text: `#FFFFFF`
- Accent / Chrome Silver: `#C0C0C0` to `#E8E8E8` gradient (metallic, like a polished car badge)
- Gradient usage: Silver-to-white gradients on headings, borders, and CTA buttons. Think chrome car emblem, not flat grey.

**Typography:**
- Headings: `Bebas Neue` or `Barlow Condensed` — bold, wide, uppercase
- Body: `Inter` or `DM Sans` — clean, modern
- Letter-spacing on headings: generous (0.08–0.12em)

**Logo:**
- Display the LGF logo (user will provide image file: `lgf-logo.png`) in the navbar and hero. The logo is 3D chrome metallic lettering on black — respect that aesthetic throughout.

**Overall vibe references:**
- ciridae.com — dark, editorial, full-bleed imagery
- nayapay.com/business — bold gradients, 3D elements, confident layout
- basicagency.com — typographic punch, cinematic white space
- dayy.com — smooth scroll, premium feel

---

### SECTIONS (in order)

---

#### 1. NAVBAR
- Fixed, transparent on load → becomes solid black with a subtle silver bottom border on scroll
- Left: LGF logo (image)
- Right: nav links — About, Services, Work, Partners, Contact
- On mobile: hamburger menu with full-screen black overlay
- Smooth scroll to sections on click

---

#### 2. HERO SECTION
- Full viewport height (`100vh`)
- **Left side:** Large bold headline text (2 lines):
  - Line 1: `"WE SHOOT"` — massive, white
  - Line 2: `"WHAT OTHERS CAN'T."` — same size, silver chrome gradient
- Subtext below: `"Automobile. Tactical. Commercial. — Karachi, Pakistan"`
- A single CTA button: `"See Our Work"` → smooth scroll to portfolio. Style: silver gradient border, black fill, white text, hover fills with silver gradient
- **Right side:** A full-height, full-bleed hero car photograph (user will swap in: placeholder a dark moody sports car shot). Add a subtle right-to-left dark vignette gradient so text stays readable.
- Subtle fade-up animation on text load (GSAP)

---

#### 3. ABOUT LGF
- Black background
- Left: A vertical silver chrome line accent, then bold heading: `"LOW GRADE FILMS"` with a paragraph below explaining they are a Karachi-based creative production house, experts in making machines, metal, and firepower look extraordinary on camera. Keep copy punchy and confident — 3–4 sentences max.
- Right: A moody BTS photo or a second car detail shot (placeholder image slot)
- Fade-in on scroll (GSAP ScrollTrigger)

---

#### 4. SERVICES
- Black background
- Section heading: `"WHAT WE DO"` — chrome gradient, centered, large
- 3 service cards in a row (stack on mobile):

  **Card 1 — Automobile**
  - Icon: a stylized car silhouette (SVG or emoji-free Unicode symbol)
  - Title: `AUTOMOBILE PHOTOGRAPHY & FILM`
  - Desc: "Ferraris, muscle cars, supercars — we make every machine look like it belongs on a poster."

  **Card 2 — Guns & Tactical**
  - Icon: crosshair / scope SVG
  - Title: `TACTICAL & FIREARMS PHOTOGRAPHY`
  - Desc: "Precision. Power. We capture the craft behind every firearm with cinematic intensity."

  **Card 3 — Commercial**
  - Icon: camera / lens SVG
  - Title: `COMMERCIAL & BRAND SHOOTS`
  - Desc: "Product launches, brand campaigns, content creation — built for impact."

- Card style: dark charcoal `#0D0D0D` background, silver gradient top border, subtle hover lift (translateY -6px, silver glow)
- Staggered fade-in on scroll

---

#### 5. PORTFOLIO / GALLERY — HORIZONTAL CINEMATIC STRIP
- Section heading: `"THE WORK"` — left-aligned, massive, chrome gradient
- A horizontally scrollable strip of photos that the user drags/scrolls through
- Cards are tall and narrow (roughly 320px × 480px), slight gap between them
- On hover: scale up slightly (1.04), overlay fades in with the photo name/category in silver text
- Photos: user will provide from their Google Drive shoot. Use 8–10 placeholder `<img>` slots with `object-fit: cover`
- Implement mouse-drag scroll (JS: `mousedown` + `mousemove` drag behavior) AND touch scroll
- Scroll-triggered fade-in for the section heading; strip itself is drag-scrollable

---

#### 6. PARTNER LOGOS
- Section heading: `"TRUSTED BY"` — centered, silver gradient
- A horizontally auto-scrolling logo ticker (CSS infinite marquee animation, no JS needed)
- Logos displayed in white/silver monochrome (use CSS `filter: brightness(0) invert(1)` so any colored logos become white)
- User will provide logo image files. Create 6–8 `<img>` placeholder slots
- Background: pure black, very subtle silver top and bottom 1px border lines on the ticker row

---

#### 7. CONTACT / BOOK US
- Section heading: `"LET'S CREATE SOMETHING."` — chrome gradient, centered
- Subheading: `"Drop us a message or hit us on WhatsApp — we respond fast."`
- **Left side:** A contact form with fields: Name, Email, Project Type (dropdown: Automobile / Tactical / Commercial / Other), Message. Submit button: silver gradient border, black fill, hover fills silver.
- **Right side:** A large fancy WhatsApp CTA block — dark card (`#0D0D0D`), green WhatsApp icon, bold text: `"Prefer to chat?"`, subtext: `"Message us directly on WhatsApp"`, and a styled button: `"Open WhatsApp"` linking to `https://wa.me/[PHONENUMBER]` (user replaces number). Add a subtle pulsing green glow animation on the button.
- Also include: a line with the email address (placeholder: `hello@lowgradefilms.com`)
- Fade-in on scroll

---

#### 8. FOOTER
- Minimal: LGF logo left, copyright center: `© 2025 Low Grade Films. All rights reserved.`
- Right: Instagram + YouTube icon links (SVG icons)
- 1px silver top border

---

### ANIMATIONS (GSAP + ScrollTrigger)
- All sections: `opacity: 0 → 1`, `y: 40 → 0`, `duration: 0.8`, `ease: power2.out` on scroll enter
- Hero text: staggered fade-up on page load
- Service cards: staggered (0.15s between each)
- No jarring effects — smooth, premium, cinematic

---

### MOBILE RESPONSIVENESS
- Full mobile-first responsive design
- Hamburger nav on mobile
- Services stack vertically
- Hero becomes full-bleed image background with text overlay on mobile
- Horizontal gallery remains swipeable (touch scroll)

---

### FILE PLACEHOLDERS TO SWAP

### NOTES
- No stock-looking design. Every choice should feel like it belongs in a car commercial.
- Avoid flat greys — the silver must always feel metallic, gradient, dimensional.
- No rounded corners on cards — keep edges sharp and automotive.
- Whitespace is intentional. Don't crowd the layout.
